Driver Improvement Program

A Driver Improvement Program is a program intended to help drivers take responsibility in improving their driving behavior, increasing safety, and reduce the risk of future crashes. It typically involves education and learning activities that focus on driver attitude and techniques needed to stay safe on the road. Programs may include classroom instruction, driver simulation training, or online instructional materials.

Alcohol and Drug Program

Alcohol and Drug Programs are designed to help people with substance abuse issues make changes in their lives. These programs involve education, skills training, support groups, and other services aimed at helping individuals learn about the effects of alcohol or drugs on their bodies, develop healthier habits and lifestyles, and ultimately become drug-free. The ultimate goal is to bring each individual back to a state of healthier functioning.
